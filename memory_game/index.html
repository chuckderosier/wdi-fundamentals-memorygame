Instructions

Concentration, also known as Memory Match, Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkei-suijaku, Pexesco or simply Pairs, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are fliped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards, which are then removed. When all the cards are gone YOU WIN!

Bonus challenge: keep track of how many times you had to turn cards face up and try to win with the least amount of card turn overs!
